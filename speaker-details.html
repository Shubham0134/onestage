About One Stage

One Stage is a foremost Artist & Event Management Company in India. One Stage is the premiere service for bridging the gap between business and the artist. We offer expertise based solution and Artist coordination & management services anywhere in India. Our team of Artist management deal with a Celebrity/Artist promotional events for a, -Weddings, College Events, Corporate Events, Festival, Sports Events, Mall Promotions, Brand Endorsement, Film /Product launch/ Branding, Factory/ Branch/ Real Estate Project Inauguration/ Ribbon-Cutting Ceremony, Road Shows/ Campaigning, Theme Parties/ Annual Celebration/ Housewarming events, Any singing, Dancing events, etc. Where Celebrities are the center of attraction for their style and charisma. We organize Live concerts/ Fashion shows, Award shows, Modelling & Talent shows

One Stage Event & Entertainment is all about capturing art and spreading it with love and passion. With our collaborations with Bollywood celebrities and mentors, we provide support to our members and educate them on how to make it big in the industry. From fashion weeks to pageants shows, we have left a mark everywhere.
